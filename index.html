Strategic Recommendation: DCA

Since there is no 'perfect moment', the most effective risk-mitigation strategy is Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA): invest fixed amounts regularly to smooth the entry price, increasing the amount only when indicators signal 'Fear' or 'Undervaluation'.
